ABOUT BAWLS

BAWLS ONU exists to connect the world through a shared interest in humor and mobilize communities to achieve social impact. We leverage the power of funny narratives to draw attention, then redirect that attention towards social impact.

BAWLS stands for Bravery, Awareness, Wellness, Laughter, Social Good. When communities form around a common idea the potential is limitless.
